The Three Jars Kit

Start saving this week — with the little that already comes in.

If you work hard and there's never anything left at the end of the month, take a breath: it's not a lack of discipline, and it's not your fault. Money doesn't disappear because you're careless — it disappears because it has no place to stay. This kit gives it that place. Print it, cut out the three labels and stick them on three jars (or envelopes, or boxes). The rest is just consistency.

✂️ The 3 jars — cut out and stick on

NOW
The life that can't stop: power, gas, medicine, food. Money with a set time to leave — so you're never caught off guard again.
PEACE
The emergency jar — the surprise you didn't plan for. Without it, every scare becomes debt. With it, a scare stays just a scare. This is the jar of a good night's sleep.
DREAM
The jar that gives meaning to the others. Big or small, the size doesn't matter — what matters is that it exists. A woman who saves only to pay bills gives up; a woman who saves for a dream keeps going.

— — — — — — cut on the line — — — — — —

How to split it (no spreadsheet, no hard math)

  1. Ask just this: how much can I set aside today without missing the essentials? Five dollars works. The amount isn't the point — the habit is.
  2. At first, almost everything goes into the PEACE jar. It's the foundation: the reserve that catches a fall.
  3. Once PEACE can cover a scare (you'll feel that relief in your body), what went to it now goes to the DREAM. You feed NOW whenever things get tight.
Your first step — today or tomorrow:
Set up three jars. Write the three names. And put any amount into the PEACE jar — five dollars is enough. The amount doesn't matter, the gesture does. After it, you're no longer the woman who only tried. You're the woman who started.
